About me

I’m a passionate Web Developer, I work either on frontend or backend. I like also work in such different areas like SEO, Accessibility and Project Management. I’m looking at any time for new challenges and incentives to keep myself at the cutting edge. I like to share the knowledge to help everyone whenever possible, I enjoy even the constructive co-workers competition that ends up to either knowledge-growing or product quality improvement. I like to spend my spare time to study new technologies and methodologies to be applied the next day at work.

Since the early 2000s I’ve been focused to the web development, and nowadays I’m almost totally involved with PHP and JS applications. I’ve obtained certification as PHP 5 Engineer and IWA Application Developer.

Just for passion, curiosity and interest I’ve created Bonzai, an open-source tool for encoding the PHP projects. That kept me involved over the years a lot on the study of the PHP Core, the writing of a custom extension in C and the source code analysis. In the last years I was committed on two big project, a web portal for an healthcare company and a real-time statistic system e-commerce oriented, by these projects I’ve got direct experience about agile development and about mentoring junior developers. I do like refactoring (sometimes just to improve either performance or scalability), documentation (not only about code), and testing that are anything but boring. I also had the opportunity to put mind and hands directly on the project management, following several aspects of those projects’ life cycle.

Lately I’ve been involved in the learning, either for fun or work, of MongoDB, Perl, Python, Redis, Xpath and Zend Framework, and I’m going to start with Arduino, Cacti, Doctrine 2, Node.js, Raspberry Pi, Symfony 2 and mod_pagespeed.
